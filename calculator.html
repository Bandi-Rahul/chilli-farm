F — Tips for Best Price

🏪 Sell at Hubli Amaragol APMC — ₹5,000–10,000 more per quintal vs Sindhanur. Transport: ₹500–800/Q. Worth it for 10+ quintals.
📅 Best window: January 2027 — Sell 40–50% in January. Peak processor demand before Rabi stock arrives.
❄️ Cold storage saves money — If price below ₹14,000/Q, store 2–3 months. Cost: ₹1,000–2,000/Q. Can gain ₹3,000–8,000 extra later.
🌿 Quality = Higher Price — Harvest red-ripe, sun-dry to 8–10% moisture. Quality 2043 gets ₹5,000–8,000 extra per quintal.
📱 Check KRAMA daily — Visit krama.karnataka.gov.in before deciding to sell. Don't trust trader's word alone.
