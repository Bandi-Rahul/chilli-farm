Year-on-Year — Peak Season Price (January each year)

Q1 Jan–Mar = best selling window
📈 2021→2023: Bull Run (+90%)
Reduced arrivals + oleoresin export surge drove 2043 from ₹9,500→₹18,000 and 5531 to ₹22,000. SpiceExtra confirmed lower arrivals caused Q1 2023 peak.
📉 2023→2024: Crash (−25 to −32%)
SpiceExtra confirmed: Feb 2024 arrivals +100% vs prev year. Farmers who planted extra after 2023 highs caused their own price crash. 5531 fell ₹22K→₹15K.
⚠️ 2025: Deepest Trough
Bumper AP/TG crop. 2043 touched ₹7,000/Q off-season (Aug 2025, KisanDeals). Even Q1 2025 was only ₹13,000 — near breakeven at ₹90K/acre costs.
🔮 2026: Recovery Underway
Q1 2026 Sindhanur peak: ₹24,100 (Feb 11). Area reduction starting. Cold stock low. Base prediction Q1 2027: ₹16,000–22,000 for 2043 (+39% from today).

Seasonal Price Cycle — 5-Year Average by Quarter

Which 3 months gives best price?
🟢 Q1 (Jan–Mar) — SELL HERE
Peak prices every year. Sell 40–50% of crop in January.
🟡 Q4 (Oct–Dec) — GOOD
Pre-harvest recovery. Sell green chilli / first dry pickings here.
🟠 Q2 (Apr–Jun) — AVERAGE
Arrivals flood market and prices fall. Sell early in this window.
🔴 Q3 (Jul–Sep) — WORST
Off-season lows. Use cold storage if possible. 2043 touched ₹7,000 here in 2025.
